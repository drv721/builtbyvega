⚙️ Full Service vs. Strategy + Branding Only
This is a fundamental business model decision that needs to be made before writing site copy.

Full service = Dan executes campaigns, manages vendors, produces deliverables, runs V monitoring. Higher billing but more time-intensive.

Strategy + branding only = Dan builds the plan and brand, client executes. Lower billing, more scalable, better for solo operation.

Tiered hybrid = Strategy at T1, strategy + some execution at T2, full service at T3.

The research sprint (P1, Prompt 2) gives you market data on what clients actually expect. Decide after that sprint, not before.
📋 SMB + Nonprofit Needs Defined
Needs to be answered before site copy and service page. Research sprint P1, Prompt 3 covers this specifically.

SMBs typically need: Brand building, lead gen, content strategy, marketing ops setup, sponsorship/partnership strategy.

Nonprofits typically need: Donor communications, community partnerships, cause marketing, grant positioning, volunteer recruitment campaigns.

Both need: Integrated campaign thinking and strategic leadership at a price point they can afford.

Run the research sprint to validate these assumptions against current market data before locking service specs.
