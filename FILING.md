## Naming Conventions

- **Folders:** No spaces — use hyphens (`Brand-Assets/`) or run words together
- **Files:** `ProjectName-Type-YYYY-MM-DD.ext` → e.g., `BbV-Brand-Brief-2026-05-09.docx`
- **Versions:** `-v2`, `-v3` — never "copy", "final", or "FINAL"
- **Existing files:** Don't rename — only new files follow this convention
